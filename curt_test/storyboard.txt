## STORYBOARD: THE LAST LINE (REVISION #13)

---
SCENE 1: INT. DUSTY OFFICE - NIGHT

Shot Type: Extreme Close-Up (ECU)
Camera Movement: Static
Camera Angle: Eye Level (focused on the keys)
Composition: The frame is dominated by the glossy black keys of the vintage typewriter. A single, harsh cone of yellow light illuminates the center, catching dust motes swirling above the letter 'A' and 'S'.
Lighting: Hard, directional tungsten light from the desk lamp, creating deep shadows around the edges of the frame. Creates a sense of isolation.
Color Palette: High contrast blacks, deep shadows, warm yellow highlights.
Action: The keys are perfectly still. The ambient sound of rhythmic rain begins faintly.
Visual Focus: The texture of the keys and the dancing dust motes.
Duration: ~3 seconds

Shot Type: Medium Close-Up (MCU)
Camera Movement: Static
Camera Angle: Eye Level
Composition: Martha is framed from the chest up, leaning slightly over the typewriter. Her intense eyes are visible, staring intently at the blank paper in the carriage. The harsh lamp casts strong shadows across her face, highlighting the tension around her eyes and mouth.
Lighting: Harsh, low-key lighting; the background recedes into near-total darkness.
Color Palette: Muted browns, deep blacks, and the stark white of the paper.
Action: Martha stares, her expression strained. She speaks her line.
Visual Focus: Martha's intense, focused eyes.
Duration: ~4 seconds

Shot Type: Extreme Close-Up (ECU)
Camera Movement: Static (Slight, momentary push-in on the final key strike)

Lighting: Bright, focused overhead light.
Color Palette: Stark white paper contrast against dark grey/black keys.
Action: Martha types the word "Never." Her fingers hit the keys with aggressive force. She immediately slams the backspace key repeatedly (*click-clack*).
Visual Focus: The violent motion of the keys being struck and then violently reversed.
